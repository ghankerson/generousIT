WHAT WE DO
 


Access Sharing

There exist today a divide between those who have access to technology and those who don't. The Digital Divide. How would your dreams change if you had access to state of the art technology? Fast internet connections, 3D printers, the best video/media production software, and a network of passionate professions. We hope to acquire digital and technology assets and share it with those who don't have access


Digital Literacy Advocacy

Digital literacy is the ability to effectively and critically navigate, evaluate and create information using a range of digital technologies. It allows people to interact, communicate and connect on a regular basis. The knowledge and use of computers was once thought to be a specialty. It now is a necessity. We hope to advocate for more opportunities and education in technology.


Youth/Asset Development

Take a look in the public library. Chances are you will find youth on computers playing video games, browsing Facebook, or watching videos. They are digital consumers. We hope to train youth to become digital producers. Whether it is creating a HTML5 website, editing photos on Photoshop, mixing music, or contributing on a blog, youth are a valuable asset and can contribute to the world today! We must raise up responsible digital citizens.
